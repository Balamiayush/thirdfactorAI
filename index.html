Frequently Asked Questions

Can the ThirdFactor KYC application work as a plug-and-play solution?

Yes. Our KYC system features a self-hosted architecture that integrates seamlessly with your existing infrastructure. The highly customizable system can be configured to your specific requirements while allowing quick deployment of advanced identity verification capabilities without extensive development work.

Can the KYC solution be hosted 100% on internal servers?

Absolutely. Our solution is designed for complete on-premises hosting, ensuring compliance with Nepal's data protection laws. This approach keeps sensitive customer information within your secure environment, reinforces data sovereignty, and provides greater flexibility for integration with your existing systems.

What is the cost model for the ThirdFactor KYC application?

We offer a flexible pricing structure with a one-time implementation fee covering customization,integration, and training, followed by annual licensing that includes updates and support. Our tiered packages accommodate organizations of all sizes, and our stateless implementation eliminates additional data storage costs. Contact us for a customized quote.

How long does it take for a verification journey to complete?

The default verification process takes approximately 15 minutes, covering facial recognition, liveness detection, document processing, and data validation. This duration is customizable based on your requirements and the complexity of verification needed for different customer segments.
